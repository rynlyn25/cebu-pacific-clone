I. Cebu Pacific Membership Program

MyCebuPacific is Cebu Pacific’s free membership program, owned and administered by Cebu Air, Inc. wherein members can securely create and manage their profile details, flights and booking information, as well as preferences for booking, and notifications, regarding their Cebu Pacific flights. Members may also store credit card information for faster and easier booking. The information is encrypted by Cebu Pacific’s payment gateway partner, which is compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard.

Upon creation of a member profile, a member confirms to have read, understood and agreed to be bound by these Terms and Conditions, including all amendments as may be made by Cebu Pacific in its sole discretion, periodically.

Cebu Pacific reserves the right to amend these Terms and Conditions at any time by posting a notice and a revised version of the Terms and Conditions on the Cebu Pacific website(s). The revisions shall be effective thirty (30) days after it is posted in the website and it is the responsibility of the members to be apprised of these changes. Your continued use of this website and/or the program following the posting of changes and/or modifications will constitute your acceptance of the revised Terms and Conditions.

Purchase of Cebu Pacific flights, products, and/or services shall be governed by the terms and conditions of the product and/or service and Cebu Pacific’s General Terms and Conditions of Carriage of Passengers and Baggage, as applicable.

II. Membership Guidelines

Guests can register for their MyCebuPacific account at any time through the Cebu Pacific website or mobile app. A MyCebuPacific account is for individuals aged eighteen (18) years old and above only. No corporations, partnerships or other legal entities are eligible to enroll as members.

An account will only be considered active once the user verifies his/her email address used in the account’s creation. By activating the account, the user will be able to utilize all benefits and features accessible only through this program. All MyCebuPacific accounts are free of charge.

All newly activated accounts will need to complete the Multi-factor Authentication process. The user will need to indicate and verify the primary email address and mobile number of the MyCebuPacific account. This will be used to verify the user’s activities with the Cebu Pacific membership program and all changes to a member’s profile information or booking/s.

Each member will only have access to his/her own account as membership is pursuant to Cebu Pacific’s Data Privacy Policy.

III. Enrollment and Registration Procedures

Guests can sign-up for a MyCebuPacific account through the Cebu Pacific website or mobile app by clicking “Log in” from the menu and proceeding to “Sign up”. Guests can choose to enter all required information or sign up through their Facebook, Google, or Go Rewards account. Once all details are completed, guests will receive an activation link in the email provided in MyCebuPacific.

IV. Linking Social Accounts

Guests may also register for a MyCebuPacific account using their social media accounts, including but not limited to – Facebook, Google, Go Rewards. By doing so, members may be able to utilize certain features accessible only through other social media accounts.

V. Features and Privileges

Members of MyCebuPacific can enjoy a wide range of features and privileges. Each member will have access to his/her own account information, which includes his/her basic information and preferences, which may be used as pre-set information for future bookings. It will also be easier for members to see their past and current bookings and make revisions, if necessary. Members will have visibility on his/her pending cases escalated to the Cebu Pacific Customer Care Team and on his/her Travel Fund balance, if applicable. Additionally, members may be notified of travel alerts, seat sales, and other promotions.

Members can also create guest lists to easily save the personal information of their companion/s, which may be used for faster booking creation, wherein majority of the required fields may be automatically filled-up. Members will have the capability to manage the information stored in their account and during the booking process.

It will also be easier for members to see the list of their past and future bookings; manage them with ease; and set booking preferences so that the next time the member books a Cebu Pacific flight, it will be faster and easier on any channel whether it is through the website, the mobile app, or the Cebu Pacific chatbot on Facebook.

Cebu Pacific reserves the right to modify, add, or remove features and privileges at its sole discretion.

VI. Inactive Membership Account

A MyCebuPacific account will be considered inactive if there is no activity with any Cebu Pacific channel for one (1) year. The data will be maintained and the user will still get all regular communications and notifications channeled through MyCebuPacific.

If the MyCebuPacific account remains to be inactive for a continuous period of three (3) years, then the MyCebuPacific account and corresponding user’s information will be deleted from the system.

VII. Account Deactivation and Deletion

  • Account Deactivation

By deactivating a MyCebuPacific account, the user explicitly agrees to stop all processing of profile, flight, and booking information and data. Additionally, all forms of communication and notifications related to the MyCebuPacific account will likewise cease. All data relating and owned by the MyCebuPacific account will remain within the Cebu Pacific database, still adhering to its Privacy Policy.

A user may re-activate his/her existing MyCebuPacific Membership account anytime.

  • Account Deletion

By deleting a MyCebuPacific account, the user explicitly agrees to delete and purge all data and information owned and relating to the Cebu Pacific membership profile from the systems of Cebu Pacific and its partners or subsidiaries. However, accounts with pending bookings cannot be deleted.

The account deletion will be completed within seven (7) business days from the time the request is made. A user may not re-claim a MyCebuPacific account once it has been deleted.

VIII. Account Transfers

Members cannot merge two (2) or more MyCebuPacific accounts, but they can link different social media accounts (i.e. Facebook, Google) to their MyCebuPacific profile. Members cannot transfer data from one account to another.

IX. Data Privacy

Cebu Pacific’s Privacy Policy shall apply suppletorily to the MyCebuPacific program.

X. Participation in the Go Rewards Loyalty Program

Cebu Pacific participates as a core coalition partner in the Go Rewards loyalty program. If you are a Go Rewards member, you acknowledge and agree that, by availing yourself of the products and/or services offered by Cebu Pacific, you may receive rewards, benefits, and offers linked to your program membership or transactions with program partners, in accordance with the Go Rewards Terms and Conditions and the Go Rewards Privacy Notice.

Cebu Pacific Policies

1. Communication to passengers when there are changes to flights
Cebu Pacific aims to keep passengers informed, especially when there are changes to their flights, so our passengers are able to make alternative plans:

  • Planned disruption – Flight schedules may be changed due to several reasons. When this happens, Cebu Pacific will send an SMS and/or email notification to affected passengers, using the contact details they have provided in their booking. Similarly, depending on the situation, notifications may also be provided through Cebu Pacific's social networking sites, and corporate website.
  • On-the-spot disruption – Flights may encounter delays or cancellation during the day of the flight due to environmental, operational, or technical reasons. When this happens, notification is provided by the relevant frontline team and/or through automated SMS and email.

2. Provision of options to passengers with changes to flights
Cebu Pacific will ensure customer’s welfare is taken care of whenever there are planned or on-the-spot changes to flights. Below are scenarios and options that passengers have, as aligned with the Philippine Air Passenger Bill of Rights:

  • Schedule change of less than one hour – When flight schedule is changed by less than one hour, passengers will not be given any flexibility options.
  • Schedule change of one hour or longer – When flight schedule is changed by one hour or longer, passengers will be given the following options:
    • Rebooking for travel within thirty (30) days, waived fare difference and any applicable penalty fees; or
    • Conversion to Travel Fund.
  • Flight cancellation more than 7 days before scheduled time of departure – When flight is cancelled more than 7 days before scheduled time of departure, whether attributable to Cebu Pacific or not, passengers will be given the following options:
    • Rebooking for travel thirty (30) days, waived fare difference and any applicable penalty fees; or
    • Conversion to Travel Fund; or
    • Refund according to original form of payment.
  • Flight cancellation of 24 hours or up to 7 days before scheduled time of departure, attributable to Cebu Pacific – When flight is cancelled at least 24 hours or up to 7 days before scheduled time of departure, passengers will be given the following options:
    • Rebooking for travel within thirty (30) days, waived fare difference and any applicable penalty fees;
    • Conversion to Travel Fund; or
    • Refund according to original form of payment.
  • Flight cancellation less than 24 hours before scheduled time of departure, attributable to Cebu Pacific – When flight is cancelled less than 24 hours before scheduled time of departure, passengers will be given a one-way Travel Voucher equivalent to the sector cancelled. They will also be provided the following options:
    • Rebooking for travel thirty (30) days, waived fare difference and any applicable penalty fees;
    • Conversion to Travel Fund; or
    • Refund according to original form of payment. In addition, passengers who are already in the airport at the time of cancellation may be provided with refreshments, meals, or a food voucher equivalent to such.
  • Flight cancellation due to other causes – When flight is cancelled due to force majeure, safety and/or security reasons, passengers will be given the following options:
    • Rebooking for travel within thirty (30) days, waived fare difference and any applicable penalty fees;
    • Conversion to Travel Fund or,
    • Refund according to original form of payment.
  • Terminal delay of at least two (2) hours after scheduled time of departure – Whether delay is attributable to Cebu Pacific or not, passengers who encounter flight delay of two (2) hours or more after scheduled time of departure will be provided with refreshments, meals, or a food voucher equivalent to such.
  • Terminal delay of at least four (4) hours after scheduled time of departure, attributable to Cebu Pacific – Passengers who encounter flight delay of at least four (4) hours after the scheduled time of departure will be given a one-way Travel Voucher equivalent to the sector delayed, as well as refreshments, meals, or a food voucher, as applicable. They will also be provided one of the following options:
    • Rebooking for travel within thirty (30) days, waived fare difference and any applicable penalty fees;
    • Conversion to Travel Fund, or;
    • Refund according to original form of payment.

    For flights departing from Vietnam, eligible passengers may choose between the options above and Vietnam-regulated compensation of USD 50 in cash or a one-way Travel Voucher equivalent to the delayed sector. The Travel Voucher may be issued only with the passenger’s prior written consent. Compensation shall be provided immediately at the airport, where practicable, or within fourteen (14) working days for bank transfer.

  • Tarmac delays of two (2) hours or more – Passengers, on best effort and availability, will be given food and beverages, no later than two (2) hours after the closing of doors (for departing flights) or after landing (for arriving flights), unless there are safety and security constraints. As needed, priority will be given to passengers with special needs, small children, senior citizens, and expectant mothers. Cebu Pacific shall also endeavor to provide access to communication, operational lavatories, air conditioning, and make available onboard emergency medical services.

    Learn more about Contingency Plan for Long Tarmac Delays here.

  • Missed connecting flight with Cebu Pacific, if with sum of sectors (SOS) – Passengers with SOS booking, whose connecting flight (second flight) has been missed due to disruption of the first flight will be given the same options as flight cancellation or delays, accordingly. Whichever option is selected for the first leg shall be applied to the connecting flight (second flight). If only the second leg is disrupted, then the flight disruption options will be applicable to that leg only.
  • Missed connecting flight with Cebu Pacific, if not SOS but multi-sectoral – Passengers with multi-sectoral flights in one booking or those with multiple bookings, whose connecting flight (second flight) has been missed due to disruption of the first flight will be given the same options as flight cancellation or delays, accordingly. The option selected for the first flight may be different from the option to be selected for the connecting flight (second flight), for which case, the following are the passenger’s options:
    • Rebooking for travel within thirty (30) days, waived fare difference and any applicable penalty fees;
    • Conversion to Travel Fund or,
    • Refund according to original form of payment.
    • Transfer to another airline without paying any fare difference.
  • Missed flight with another airline – Passengers who miss their connecting flights on another airline due to Cebu Pacific’s flight cancellation or delay will not be compensated by Cebu Pacific. We can, however, provide a certificate of flight disruption (COFD) for whatever purpose it may serve, should passengers request for documentation.
  • Overbooking and Denied Boarding – In the event that a flight is overbooked, the Ground Operations Team will look for passengers who are willing to be voluntarily denied boarding and transferred to the next available Cebu Pacific flight. In addition to a confirmed seat, they will receive a round-trip Travel Voucher equivalent to the sector of the original booking. If necessary, passengers may be provided access to amenities, which include food and drinks, hotel accommodations, etc.

    In situations where there are not enough volunteers, passengers may be denied boarding based on the following priority list:
    • Unaccompanied minors
    • Senior citizens, PWDs and their companion(s)
    • Passengers traveling with children under four (4) years old
    • Passengers with scheduled non-elective medical procedures
    • Passengers who were previously denied boarding on the same ticket
    • Passengers who have outward connecting flights Revenue passengers and non-revenue passengers who booked using a Travel Voucher and are denied boarding will receive cash compensation in addition to the last offer made to passengers who volunteered to be denied boarding.

3. Best effort to depart on time
We will do our best to depart on time. Being on time is defined as flights departing within fifteen (15) minutes from published departure time. We work closely with the airport authorities, as airport control towers must give airlines clearance before we can take off and land, among other procedures. Here are the measures in place to ensure this:

  • Check-in/Bag Drop Counter closure – We have standardized counter closure to finalize the documents needed for the flight. This includes the final passenger manifest and weight and balance documents of the flight. Check-in/Bag Drop counter closure is one (1) hour before the scheduled time of departure for international flights and forty-five (45) minutes before the scheduled time of departure for domestic flights.
  • Boarding priority – For an organized and faster boarding process and in consideration to passengers with special needs, we will prioritize passengers carrying infants, expectant mothers, senior citizens and passengers with disability. Passengers who purchased paid seats with priority boarding will also be called to board first, when applicable.
  • Boarding gate closure - Cebu Pacific may offload passengers who are not yet in the boarding gate before the scheduled departure time. This is so everyone else who boarded the flight on time, can also depart on time. Boarding gate closure is fifteen (15) minutes before scheduled time of departure.

4. Delivery of baggage on time
Cebu Pacific endeavors that all checked baggage arrives at your destination. In case of baggage irregularity, please be guided by the following:

  • Delayed Baggage – Passengers must immediately file a Property Irregularity Report (PIR) within the premises of the arrival station, if baggage did not arrive in the same flight as the passenger. Cebu Pacific will provide Php2,000 compensation to the passenger for every twenty-four (24) hours of delay. Additionally, if checked baggage is not delivered within 24 hours, the applicable baggage fees will be refunded.
  • Damaged Baggage – Passengers must immediately file a Property Irregularity Report (PIR) while still at the airport premises of the arrival station, if the baggage was damaged. If damage is proven not to be caused by regular wear and tear, we will compensate based on the Philippine Air Passenger Bill of Rights or the Montreal Convention.
  • Lost Baggage - Baggage that is not found within seven (7) days from date of arrival will be considered lost. Cebu Pacific will provide Php2,000 compensation to the passenger for every twenty-four (24) hours of delay until baggage is considered lost. Compensation is based on the Philippine Air Passenger Bill of Rights or the Montreal Convention. We will make every effort to return mishandled baggage within twenty-four (24) hours and will reimburse affected passenger for any fee charged to transport a bag.

5. Confirmed booking with amount due on the day flight
It is recommended that guests have already paid for the entire balance using any of the payment channels before the flight. Cebu Pacific provides guests with different payment options, such as:

  • Cash
  • Credit Card / Debit Card
  • Agent Accounts
  • E-Wallets (e.g., GCash, GrabPay, etc.)
  • Travel Fund

Should there be any balance on the day of flight, guests will have to pay for the remaining balance in the airport, within the allowed cut-off time before flight departure. Otherwise, they will be denied check-in and boarding to the flight.

7. Baggage handling and limitations
Guests can bring the following types of baggage during flights:

  • Checked Baggage: Guests can check in their baggage, given that they have availed of baggage allowance before their flight. Guests can purchase up to three (3) pieces of 20kg prepaid baggage allowance. Each bag may be upgraded in increments of 4kg (up to 12kg). Strollers and walking canes can be checked in for free. For safety reasons, maximum weight per checked baggage is 32kg.
  • Carry-on baggage – Each guest is allowed one (1) piece of carry-on bag that fits the overhead bin and one (1) bag that fits under the seat in front. Carry-on baggage is subject to the following size and weight restrictions:
    • Total carry-on baggage must not exceed 7 kg.
    • Dimensions for carry-on baggage must not exceed 56 cm x 36 cm x 23 cm.
    • Some examples of a hand-carry item include: Cabin-size trolley/luggage, Backpack or laptop/camera bag, Sling bag, handbag, shoulder bag or small purse, Items purchased inside the airport, including Duty Free Items (DFI), donuts, fruit boxes, etc., Baby bag, medical-related items or food items not accepted for check-in.
  • Special baggage – Items that are larger than usual or are irregular in shape are classified as special baggage. Guests should purchase a separate baggage allowance apart from Checked Baggage. This may include but are not limited to sports equipment, surfboards, musical instruments, oversized bags, etc.

8. Refund processing
All Cebu Pacific bookings are non-refundable, unless otherwise stated. Passengers with disrupted flights must request for refund within thirty (30) days from the date of disruption notification. Once requested, refund will be processed in their original form of payment, subject to fare rules. Below are additional information depending on the form of payment used:

  • Credit / debit card or Cash or e-Wallet – If paid directly to Cebu Pacific, which includes payments made through digital channels and airport cashiers, refund can be requested via the Cebu Pacific website or mobile app, or through the 24/7 customer service channels. Due to different billing cycles, refund may take up to two (2) billing cycles to reflect in the credit / debit card account, after successful request and submission of complete requirements. If paid through travel agency, online aggregators or group reservations, passengers should directly coordinate with their respective agents.
  • Travel Fund – While Travel Fund is non-refundable, it can be used as a form of payment for booking a new flight and purchasing add-ons. Additionally, the Travel Fund may be used to pay for bookings for MyCebuPacific accountholder’s family members or friends.
  • Go Rewards – Bookings purchased with Go Rewards may be refunded to guest in the form of Travel Fund. For payments made to purchase add-ons through Cebu Pacific Partners (i.e., insurance, hotels, getaways), refund may be directly coordinated with them via their respective customer service hotlines.

9. Guests needing special assistance

Cebu Pacific embraces inclusivity and diversity of guests, regardless of gender, age, race, or status. While Cebu Pacific generally accepts everyone onboard, safety remains to be our top priority. Any guest who poses a risk to the safety of other guests or Cebu Pacific crew members will be denied boarding.

Guests who have special needs or attention, including those with disabilities, will be given the level of respect, and care they require. Guests must inform Cebu Pacific at least two (2) hours beforehand and prepare necessary documents and identification, so that we can make the necessary arrangements. Cebu Pacific will endeavor to provide special lanes and priority to them, where applicable. The medical certificate should give clearance for the guest to travel by air. The same document is required for new mothers and expectant mothers with age of gestation at thirty-four (34) weeks and beyond. A medical certificate is only valid for ten (10) days. Flight Connections and Disruptions

Updated as of May 28, 2026

Flight Connections

If you are booking two flights separately (for example, Cebu-Manila then Manila-Hong Kong), please ensure you have the minimum number of hours before the next departure.

  • If traveling within the Philippines only, allot at least two (2) hours between flights
  • If connecting to or from an international flight, allot at least three (3) hours between flights

These are the minimum time required to collect baggage and complete check-in procedures required for your second flight.

We are a point-to-point carrier, focused on flying you to your destination on our network. Passengers with Cebu Pacific misconnections brought about by Cebu Pacific delays or cancellations will be re-accommodated on the next available Cebu Pacific flights.

Should you have connecting flights with other airlines, we will not be liable for any misconnections brought about by delays, cancellations, and any flight arrangements you may choose to make.

Flight Disruptions

At any time after a booking has been made, we may change our schedules and/or cancel, terminate, divert, postpone or reschedule or delay any flight where we reasonably consider this to be justified by circumstances beyond our control or for reasons of safety and security.

In the event of these disruptions, you may choose from the following options:

  • Rebooking
  • Refund
  • Travel Fund

Fare rules and airline policies shall apply during these circumstances.
