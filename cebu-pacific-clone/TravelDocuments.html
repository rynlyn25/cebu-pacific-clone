Travel Requirements for Domestic and International Travel

Most domestic destinations are open for tourists, including children, but we recommend checking with your destination's local government unit (LGU) for the latest travel guidelines and requirements. Continue to wear a face mask inflight.

Meanwhile, guests exiting the Philippines must register for eTravel not earlier than 72 hours before the flight and not later than 3 hours before the flight. Note that proof of vaccination and/or negative COVID-19 test, among other documents, may be required to complete registration.
